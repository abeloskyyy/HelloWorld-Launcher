# Privacy Policy for HelloWorld Launcher

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

At HelloWorld Launcher, we take your privacy seriously. This document explains what information we collect, how it's used, and your rights.

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect
Currently, HelloWorld Launcher does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.
* **Credentials:** All authentication is handled directly through Microsoft/Xbox Live services using official protocols. Your password never touches our servers.
* **Files:** We do not scan or upload your local Minecraft files.

## 2. Future Data Collection (Planned)
In future versions, we may implement basic account management to improve the app with skins and more. This may include:
* Usernames.
* E-mails.
* Minecraft user skins.

If implemented, these features will be always private and stored in secure Google Firebase servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
* **GitHub:** For hosting the project and downloads.
* **Microsoft/Mojang:** For game authentication and assets.
* **SignPath Foundation:** For code signing and authenticity verification.

## 4. Your Rights
Since we currently do not store personal data, there is no data to delete. However, if data collection is implemented in the future, you will have the right to request access to or deletion of your information.
